Travel

Getting THere Is Half The Fun

Traveling to our island wedding will be slightly more involved compared to a mainland wedding; however, the San Juan Islands are beautiful and the quintessential Pacific Northwest experience. We have done our best to provide the logistical information, but please do not hesitate to contact us if you need additional clarification or have any questions on how to get there.

We highly encourage guests to plan on arriving on the island no later than Thursday, August 6th in order to fully enjoy your trip and take advantage of everything the beautiful San Juans have to offer. Thank you so much for taking the time and effort to travel for our wedding... it truly means the world to us! We are very excited to share this magical place with you.
