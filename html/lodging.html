Roche Harbor Resort

Roche Harbor, WA 98250

View Map

We have a courtesy room block at Roche Harbor Resort, the wedding venue. The resort offers many different lodging options, ranging from historic hotel rooms to condominiums to houses at a variety of price points. We would highly recommend you stay at the resort; this way, you can be mere steps away from both the wedding ceremony and reception sites come Sunday! Roche Harbor's Guest Services team also provides courtesy golf carts chauffeurs to come pick you up from your resort accommodation if you don't feel like walking.

To secure room reservations at the Roche Harbor Resort, please call (360) 378-2155, ext. 4 or email groupsales@rocheharbor.com, and use the room block code 222946. It is very important that you book your accommodations by no later than June 6, 2020! This is the room block release deadline; after this date, any open rooms in the block will be made available to the general public. Even still, we'd recommend you book your accommodations around 5-7 months in advance as everything tends to book up quickly in the summer months.

Information on the different types of lodging within the resort can be found below. Click here if you'd like to look at alternative accommodation options outside of Roche Harbor.

Roche Harbor Resort Lodging Types

Hotel De Haro

Historic hotel built in 1886 featuring antique furnishings and a comfortable environment in the center of the resort. Six suites with sitting areas and private bathrooms. Thirteen guestrooms with access to four single person bathrooms in the hallway. Guestrooms are approximately 100 SF including a king or queen size bed, desk, dresser, sink, and mirror. Sleeps up to 2 people. Click here for more details.

  • Guestrooms: $192-$214/night plus tax
  • Suites: $291-345/night plus tax

Quarryman Hall

Roche Harbor's newest addition, this boutique hotel features four deluxe suites, six standard king suites, and two double queen suites. Located in the center of the resort, it features modern amenities, elevator access, balconies, flat screen TVs, gas fireplaces, and heated tile floors. Sleeps up to 4 people. Click here for more details.

  • Double Queen: $456/night plus tax
  • Standard King: $445-456/night plus tax
  • Deluxe King: $559/night plus tax

McMillin Suites

Four deluxe king suites occupy a historic home perched on a bluff in the center of the resort. Classically decorated with comfortable sitting areas, oversize bathrooms with heated tile floors and claw-foot tubs. Sleeps up to 2 people. Click here for more details.

  • King Suite: $559/night plus tax

Habor / Lagoon View Condos

One, two, and three bedroom mini residences on the west side of our marina about six minutes' walk to the resort center. All accommodations feature full kitchens, living rooms with TV, fireplace, pull-out sleeper sofa, and as many bathrooms as there are bedrooms. Built thirty years ago, these basic, affordable units are quiet, tranquil retreats. One bedroom sleeps up to 4, two bedroom sleeps up to 6, three bedroom sleeps up to 10. Click here for more details on the Harbor View condos and here for more details on the Lagoon View condos.

Lodging Alternatives

Friday Harbor House

Located in the heart of Friday Harbor right next to the ferry terminal, the Friday Harbor House is perched atop a bluff overlooking the scenic harbor. Walking distance to all of the amenities of downtown. 20 minute drive to wedding venue.

Lakedale Resort

An 82-acre resort with three spring-water lakes, Lakedale Resort is a unique "glamping" retreat with canvas tents, log cabin rentals, and a relaxing lodge. It also has campgrounds if you want to bring your own tent for a truly rustic Northwest experience. 10 minute drive to wedding venue.

Snug Harbor Resort

Snug Harbor Resort is the perfect place to unwind and detach. The resort is made up of a series of 17 beautiful, modern waterfront cabins with expansive glass, vaulted ceilings, full kitchens, and uninterrupted, stunning views. They also have complimentary paddleboards, kayaks, bikes, and canoes available. 10 minute drive to wedding venue.

Tucker House Inn

The historic 1898 Tucker House Inn consists of a labyrinth of Victorian Buildings and three cottages and is located in downtown Friday Harbor right by the ferry terminal. With 12 guest rooms, suites and cottages, this charming, romantic inn appeals to all and is even pet-friendly. 20 minute drive to wedding venue.

Discovery Inn

One of the more budget-friendly options. Located a mile from the ferry landing, Discovery Inn features recently renovated rooms with air-conditioning, private bath, one or two queen beds, Keurig coffee maker, flat screen TV, and free Wi-Fi. 20 minute drive to wedding venue.

San Juan Island Hotel

San Juan Island Hostel is just minutes away from the ferry terminal. If you are on a budget or just enjoy meeting new people, this is the place for you. Private dorms, shared dorms, and group dorms are available, with access to a full shared kitchen and communal lounge space. 20 minute drive to wedding venue.

The Orca Inn

Another budget-friendly option, the Orca Inn features clean rooms at a low price. Seven blocks from the ferry, guest rooms include private bathrooms, microwaves, refrigerators, and A/C. 20 minute drive to wedding venue.
